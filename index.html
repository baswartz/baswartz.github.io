NES Zelda (Unity, C#)

Play Zelda

In a group of 3, recreated the original NES Zelda with a twist-- a new feature. In our Zelda, there's an extra level where the player can pick up and ride a broom, with all its new mechanics

I was responsible for most of the weapon mechanics, from the sword, bow, bombs, etc. I also created edge colliders around rooms for shooting weapons to be limited to their rooms, as well as the health system and two enemies-- keese and wallmasters.
